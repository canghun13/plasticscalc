Site operations

Contact and calculator feedback

PlasticsCalc is a static reference site. A verified public contact channel is not currently published, so this page explains what feedback is useful and how a future contact method will be introduced.

General inquiries

For partnership, editorial, or general site questions, please check this page for a verified contact method before sending any information. PlasticsCalc does not currently publish a form, mailbox, or support portal, and does not ask visitors to send confidential production or personal data.

Calculator feedback and error reports

Useful reports identify the calculator URL, entered values and units, the displayed result, expected result or reference, browser/device context, and a concise description of the issue. A public feedback channel will be added here only after it is verified and monitored.

Technical correction suggestions

Technical suggestions are most useful when they cite a machine manufacturer, resin supplier, standards body, association, textbook, or other traceable engineering source. Include the material grade, temperature basis, geometry, process condition, or definition that affects the correction.

Engineering-use reminder

Calculator results are preliminary estimates, not machine selection, mold release, safety approval, material certification, or a production setting. Review the engineering disclaimer before relying on a result for a commercial or process decision.

Privacy questions

PlasticsCalc has no accounts, checkout, or contact form. Analytics preferences can be managed from the Privacy Policy. This page will be updated if a verified privacy-contact route becomes available.

Do not include personal, confidential, customer, drawing, production, or payment information in any future feedback channel unless its privacy terms specifically state that it is appropriate.
