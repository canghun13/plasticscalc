How to Read Resin Datasheets for Molding Calculations

A source-selection guide for density, shrinkage, thermal data, regrind, and processing limits.

The engineering question

Record the exact grade, filler level, condition, temperature, test method, and whether the data is solid-state, melt-state, directional, or a supplier processing recommendation.

What to document

A generic polymer-family number is not an engineering substitute for the specified grade and condition.

Practical workflow

Use the Materials workflow before entering density, shrinkage, or cooling inputs into a calculator.

Validation boundary

Use manufacturer documentation, grade-specific supplier data, controlled process records, and trials for any equipment, quality, material, or commercial commitment. A static calculator is a transparent starting point, not a simulation or approval.
