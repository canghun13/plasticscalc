Privacy Policy

This policy describes the limited data practices of PlasticsCalc as a static information website.

Analytics

PlasticsCalc uses Google Analytics 4 only after you choose “Accept analytics” in the site preference dialog. If you decline, the site does not load the Google Analytics script. You can change this choice below at any time.

Cookies and local storage

The site stores your analytics preference locally in your browser so it can remember whether analytics is allowed. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies only after acceptance, subject to Google’s own policies.

Accounts, payments, and forms

The site does not provide user accounts, checkout, payment processing, server-side user profiles, or contact forms. We do not intentionally collect names, email addresses, or payment details through the site.

Third-party and external links

Some guides link to manufacturers, material suppliers, and technical references. Their sites have their own privacy practices. Advertising may be introduced later; this policy will be updated before any advertising technology is enabled.

Changes

We may update this page when site functionality changes. The current implementation and visitor choices should be reviewed against the requirements that apply to your location.

Privacy questions

For privacy questions about PlasticsCalc, email canghun13@naver.com. See the Contact and feedback page for the information that is useful to include.
