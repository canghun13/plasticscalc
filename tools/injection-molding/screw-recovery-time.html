Screw Recovery Time Calculator

Estimate recovery time from shot volume and measured plasticizing rate. Use this as a documented planning estimate, then validate it against the actual machine, mold, resin, and process.

Formula and method

Recovery time = shot volume / plasticizing rate.

Use a rate measured for the exact resin, back pressure, screw speed, and melt condition.

Inputs and units

Use controlled records, CAD, measured production data, the exact resin data sheet, or machine-maker specifications. Keep the material and time basis with the result.

Worked example

180 cm3 / 24 cm3/s = 7.5 s.

What the result supports

This result is a first-pass screen for process planning. Use it to identify the next record or engineering check, not as a release decision.

Sanity check

Check the unit basis and whether all quantities describe the same cavity, shot, machine, or production window before comparing values.

Important limitation

Do not substitute a catalogue maximum rate for a validated process rate.

Reference: ARBURG: cycle-time optimisation.
